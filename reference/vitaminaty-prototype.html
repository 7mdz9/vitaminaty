About This Product

Applied Nutrition Critical Whey is a UK-manufactured premium whey protein blend designed for athletes and serious trainers. Each 30g serving delivers 27g of high-quality protein, sourced from whey concentrate and isolate, with naturally occurring BCAAs and glutamine.

Critical Whey mixes smoothly with water, milk, or your shake of choice, making it ideal as a post-workout recovery shake or as a high-protein addition to your daily intake.

  • 27g protein per 30g serving
  • Blend of whey concentrate + isolate
  • Naturally occurring BCAAs & glutamine
  • Low in sugar and fat
  • UK-manufactured to GMP standards

Directions of Use

Mix 1 scoop (30g) with 250–300ml of cold water or milk in a shaker. Shake for 10–15 seconds until smooth. Best consumed within 30 minutes after training, or any time of day to support daily protein intake.

As a guide, take 1–2 servings per day depending on your training load and dietary protein needs. Do not exceed 3 servings in a 24-hour period.
